GRAY TERMINAL

グレイ・ターミナル

Known also as "不確かな物の終着駅", literally "End Station of Uncertainties", the Gray Terminal is an area located in Dawn Island just outside the row of walls that contain the Capital of Goa Kingdom.

This is the place where the unwanted goods are considered rubbish and where the Goa inhabitants eject the waste through the Great Gate.
For this reason the Gray Terminal is a lawless land in wich a lot of refugees fight for food, medical care and waste that keep them alive.

In the past, secretly, the king of Goa gave order to set fire to the whole area because the visit of some Celestial Dragons, in this way he could preserve the reputation of the reign.

After the visit the empty place returned to be a waste deposit.

Related places
