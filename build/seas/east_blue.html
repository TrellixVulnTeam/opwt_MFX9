Est Blue

The East Blue is an ocean in the Blue Sea.
Like its sister seas, it comprises almost entirely of ocean, with scattered islands and a border with the Red Line. The East Blue is commonly known as the weakest of all the seas.

Pirates who are considered great and threatening in this sea are regarded as low rate compared to pirates from the other seas and especially in the Grand Line.

Ironically, despite its poor fame, the East Blue was the birthplace of the most powerful pirate of all time, the late Pirate King Gol D. Roger.
